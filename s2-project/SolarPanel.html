Solar Panel

Course Information

  • Design for S.1 students with emphasis on water cycle (S) and ratio (M)
  • Approximate class time of 3 hours from design to build

Course Description

  • Using the youCodia platform, students will design an animation to describe the four parts of the water cycle, such as condensation, evaporation, surface run-off, and precipitation.
  • Students will have the autonomy to design the layout of the mobile app and come out with their own description of each part of the water cycle. They will apply the mathematical concept of ratio to resize their image. Making of the animation, students will learn about event handling and if-else functions. After completing the animation, students will complete an assessment for learning and watch some youtube videos on rain gauge design. They will learn about the engineering design concept of the rain gauge and how it relates to the water cycle and measure the extreme storm weather.

Learning OutCome
