S1: Pioneer ECO-STEM

CONDENSATION

When a gas turns into a liquid, ie water vapour becomes liquid.

PRECIPITATION

Liquid (rain) or solid water (snow,hail) falls to earth.

SURFACE RUNOFF

Liquid water flows across land and mountains.

EVAPORATION

When a liquid becomes a gas, ie water becomes water vapour.
