Course Information

  • Design for S.1 students with emphasis on water cycle (S) and ratio (M)
  • Approximate class time of 3 hours from design to build

Course Descripttion

  • Using the youCodia platform, students will design an animation to illustrate how water changes states between solid, liquid and gas, corresponding to the temperature. As the water changes its state, students will also illustrate the change of the water volume in their animation and explain how iceberg melting leads to water rising.
